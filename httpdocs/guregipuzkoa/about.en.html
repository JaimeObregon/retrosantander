From Gipuzkoa to the world

Gure Gipuzkoa (our Gipuzkoa) is a vast digital photographic archive that captures the visual memory of the territory and its people. A cultural, historical, and emotional legacy of portraits, landscapes, celebrations, and everyday scenes that reflect our way of life through the decades.

The project weaves together the conviction to preserve our identity with the desire to share it with the world. Each photograph becomes a window onto a story, a frozen emotion in time, a testimony of who we were and how we lived. In this endeavor, technology is an essential ally.

The project was launched in 2009 as an initiative of the Provincial Council of Gipuzkoa, through its Department of Culture. In 2025, it has been fully renewed to embrace technological innovations and offer new ways of exploring this heritage.

Gure Gipuzkoa is free software and is publicly available on GitHub, as an expression of an open, sustainable, and publicly committed model.
