The Hondarribia Town Council has its own space in the Gure Gipuzkoa portal.

The collections include protocol photographs reflecting the institutional life of the council over the last 50 years. Another collection, by Luis Mari Elosegi Aldasoro, features farmhouses photographed in 1965 and again in the 1990s, clearly showing their transformation.
