The Zaldibia Town Council shares in the Gure Gipuzkoa portal a selection of photographs from its municipal archive.

The collection is not extensive but is made available to residents and users under a Creative Commons BY-SA license.

The information about the images is brief and can be enriched with public contributions. No permission is required to use them if Zaldibia Town Council and the author are credited, and derivative works are released under the same license.

If you wish to provide additional information or corrections, the Town Council will review your proposals.
