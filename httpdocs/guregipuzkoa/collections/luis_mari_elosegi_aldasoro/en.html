Luis Mari Elosegi Aldasoro, grandson of Polikarpo Elosegi, makes available to users this photographic archive composed of two collections.

The first collection is a set of photographs of farmhouses in Hondarribia, taken by Luis Mari Elosegi Aldasoro. It includes images from 1965 and again in the mid-1990s, allowing a clear view of the changes over those years.

The second collection presents 143 photographs of farmhouses in Tolosa taken in 1961.
