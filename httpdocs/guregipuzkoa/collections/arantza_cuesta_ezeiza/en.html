This collection presents 4,000 photographs taken by historian Arantza Cuesta Ezeiza between 1991 and 1994 for the production of the Catalogue of Movable Assets of the Catholic Church in the Diocese of San Sebasti√°n. It is a selection from more than 30,000 photographs taken for the preparation of that catalogue. The collection includes images of various heritage elements, artistic and/or liturgical objects from the different chapels, churches, and roadside shrines of Gipuzkoa. Among the images are paintings, sculptures, reliefs, altarpieces, baptismal fonts, and pieces of goldsmithing, but one can also find textiles and different types of furniture.
