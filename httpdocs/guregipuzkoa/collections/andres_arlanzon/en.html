The origin of the contents shown is the photographic archive of Andrés Arlanzón, correspondent of Diario Vasco in Oñati. The materials reflect the events that took place in Oñati from the late 1950s to the early 1990s.

Among the contents that users will find in the collection, we would highlight the following:

  • Tribute to retirees
  • Inauguration of the Azkoagain field after its renovation
  • Oñati patron saint festivities
  • International Sheepdog Competition
  • Herri Eguna (People’s Day)
  • Feast of Our Lady of Arantzazu
  • Institutional visits
  • Tribute to priest Jose Maria Murua
  • Visit of Manuel Fraga
