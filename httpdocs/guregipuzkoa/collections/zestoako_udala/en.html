The Zestoa Town Council has its own space in the Gure Gipuzkoa portal.

The collections illustrate the town’s history: old postcards linked to the spa and early 20th-century tourism; photographs taken in the 1990s by historian Vicente Dávila on local heritage; and images by young residents showing the former cement factories of the Lower Urola.

Contents are published under open licenses: postcards, with no identifiable authorship, are under No Known Copyright Restrictions; the rest under Creative Commons BY-SA.

Permission is not required to use the photographs, provided attribution is given to Zestoa Town Council and the author, and any derivative work is released under the same license.

If you wish to add information, correct data or complete records, the Town Council will review your request.
