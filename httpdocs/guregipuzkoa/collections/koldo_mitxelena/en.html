The Donostia-San Sebastián Postcard Collection graphically reflects the everyday life of the city at the beginning of the 20th century. As one delves deeper into the archive, images appear that go beyond the capital: racing driver Rudolf Caracciola in his car, the entry of fascist general Mola into Donostia, superb studio portraits of fishermen, boxer Paulino Uzcudun with his automobile… endless surprises.

The collection consists of 5597 images, most of them in black and white. In addition to postcards, it also includes photographs, cards, stickers and prints.

The contents are released under two licenses: when the author is known, under Creative Commons BY-SA; when the author is unknown, under No Known Copyright Restrictions.
