Through this tool we wish to make available to users the photographic archive of Polikarpo Elosegi Ansola (Tolosa, 1870-1948).

Nephew of the founder of the Beret Factory, he was part of its management. Councillor of Tolosa Town Council in 1913, he collaborated in the organisation of the Euskaras Festivals in the town. Between 1925 and 1929 he was a director of the Basque Studies Society in the Mining and Industrial Plans Section. He co-founded the Orfeón Tolosano in 1901 and the Ski Club Tolosano in 1909. Polikarpo Elosegi married María Irazusta, with whom he had six children.

The collection consists of a total of 1066 images, all in black and white and taken between 1875 and 1942.

Among the contents managed on this site, the following can be mentioned:

  • Skiing in Azpiroz
  • Elosegui beret factory
  • Elosegi Ansola family
  • Elosegi Irazusta family
  • Old photographs of Tolosa
  • Ethnographic photographs
  • Studio portraits
